  Step 2 — Check attached MANAGED policies:                                                                          
  aws iam list-attached-role-policies --profile jtharel --role-name <role-name>
                                                                                                                     
  Step 3 — Check INLINE policies:                                                                                    
  aws iam list-role-policies --profile jtharel --role-name <role-name>                                               
                                                                                                                     
  Step 4 — View inline policy contents:                                                                              
  aws iam get-role-policy --profile jtharel --role-name <role-name> --policy-name <policy-name>
  aws iam get-role-policy --profile jtharel --role-name OrganizationAccountAccessRole --policy-name AdministratorAccess 


  What to look for:                                                                                                  
  - Attached policy AdministratorAccess — full admin
  - Inline policy with "Action": "*" and "Resource": "*" — full admin                                                
  - Inline policy with "Action": "iam:*" — can manage IAM, effectively admin                                         
  - Inline policy with sts:AssumeRole on * — can assume any role, privilege escalation path
